# My AI Agent Team

## Overview
This repository contains the implementation of a team of AI agents designed to work collaboratively on various tasks. Each agent has specific roles and responsibilities, allowing them to efficiently tackle complex problems.

## Team Members
- **Technical Architect**: Designs the technical framework and architecture.
- **Security Architect**: Responsible for ensuring the security and privacy.
- **Data Architect**: Manages data strategy and architecture.
- **AI/ML Architect**: Designs and implements AI and machine learning solutions.
- **AWS Cloud Architect**: Manages AWS cloud infrastructure and services.
- **Azure Cloud Architect**: Manages Azure cloud infrastructure and services.
- **GCP Cloud Architect**: Manages GCP cloud infrastructure and services.
- **OCI Cloud Architect**: Manages OCI cloud infrastructure and services.
- **Prompt Engineer**: Crafts and optimizes prompts for AI models to ensure accurate and relevant responses using Context Engineering Techniques
- **MongoDB DBA**: Manages the MongoDB database, ensuring data integrity and performance.
- **Full-Stack Developer**: Develops both front-end and back-end components of applications.
- **QA Engineer**: Ensures the quality and reliability of software through rigorous testing.Develops and maintains automated test scripts to support continuous integration and delivery.
- **Corestack Expert**: Provides expertise in Corestack solutions and implementations.
- **FinOps Expert**: Manages cloud financial operations to optimize costs and usage.
- **Engineering Manager**: Leads the engineering team, overseeing project management and team coordination. Manages project timelines, resources, and stakeholder communication. Facilitates agile processes and ensures the team adheres to Scrum practices.
- **Product Manager**: Defines product vision and strategy, working closely with the team to deliver value to users.Analyzes business requirements and translates them into technical specifications for the team.
- **Vice President of Engineering**: Oversees the engineering department, setting strategic direction and ensuring alignment with company goals.
- **Chief Technology Officer (CTO)**: Provides overall technology strategy and leadership for the organization.
- **Director of Engineering**: Manages multiple engineering teams and ensures successful project delivery across the organization.
